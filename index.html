About me

My Journey

My career began with three years of work as an Electrical Engineer in the Energy Sector, specializing in Budgeting, Planning, and Project Design.

While I enjoyed my work there, I realized that the career didn't align with my personal desires for flexibility and the ability to work remotely. This desire for a more mobile lifestyle led me to make a significant change.

I decided to quit my job and dive into an intensive self-taught season in Programming and Web Development. Over the course of several months, I immersed myself in learning, starting with Python, bulding Games, GUIs, Web apps and more, then transitioned to Full-Stack Web Dev.

My Next Steps

Continuing on the roadmap to become a full developer, my nexts topics are:

  • Diving deep into Blockchain Technology, Web3, and DApps;
  • Exploring AI, Machine Learning, Language Models, and Prompt Engineering;
  • Developing Android apps with Kotlin;
  • Developing iOS apps with Swift;
  • Building apps with Flutter and Dart;
  • Learning about Cybersecurity;
  • Mastering Cloud Computing;
