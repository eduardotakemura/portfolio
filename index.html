My Journey

My career began with three years of work as an Electrical Engineer in the Energy Sector, specializing in Budgeting, Planning, and Project Design of Renewable and High Voltage Projects.

While I enjoyed my work there, I realized that the career didn't align with my personal desires for flexibility and the ability to work remotely. This desire for a more mobile lifestyle led me to make a significant change.

I decided to quit my job and dive into an intensive self-taught season in Programming and Web Development. Over the course of several months, I immersed myself in learning, starting with Python, bulding Games, GUIs, Web apps and more, then transitioned to Full-Stack Web Dev.
